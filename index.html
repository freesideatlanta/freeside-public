About Freeside

Freeside is a Makerspace for hackers, designers, artists, students, and hobbyists in Atlanta, Georgia.

Freeside Technology Spaces, a 501(c)3 non-profit organization, strives to provide a space that inspires collaboration, creativity, teaching, and open projects for the Atlanta community.

We host lots of classes and projects to help people network and make their ideas a reality.
